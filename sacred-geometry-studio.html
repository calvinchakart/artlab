Rotational Division Vectors

For any layer containing $S$ structural partitions, the coordinate vertices $(v_x, v_y)$ are calculated using polar mapping rules:

$\theta_k = \frac{2\pi \cdot k}{S} + \phi_{phase} + \omega_r \cdot t$

$v_x = c_x + R_t \cdot \cos(\theta_k)$

$v_y = c_y + R_t \cdot \sin(\theta_k)$
